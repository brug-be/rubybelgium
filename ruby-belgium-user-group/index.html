Ruby Belgium User Group aka BRUG

Ruby & design talks, coding sessions & lots of fun!

Join us for the next event

We live in the Internet age, where we have so many digital channels to communicate.

Getting to know people "in real life", though, can still be very rewarding.

That's why, every third Tuesday of the month, the Ruby community is gathering in a co-working space.

The objectives are diverse: meet everybody, have a chat about common interests, listen to tech talks, or getting help with projects.

There are always beginners and seasoned developers attending, so don't be afraid to join the party, and ask for advices.
